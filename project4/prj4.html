How to Play:

  • Objective: Spot the differences between the two images in each level!
  • Hover: Hover over the image to switch between the "before" and "after" versions.
  • Time Limit: Each level has a time limit. Hover over the image to see if you can find all the differences before time runs out!
  • Levels: Play on different difficulty levels: Easy, Medium, and Hard.

Level 1: Easy

You have 5 seconds to view the items in the room. Drag the cursor out of the room once the 5 seconds are up!

Level 2: Medium

You have 7 seconds to view the items in the room. Drag the cursor out once you're done!

Level 3: Hard

Can you spot all the differences in time? Take your time but make sure to complete it within 10 seconds!
